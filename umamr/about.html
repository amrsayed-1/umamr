About UMAMR Restaurant

UMAMR Restaurant is the best place to experience authentic Egyptian food in Ireland. Our dishes are made using traditional recipes and fresh ingredients to give you a taste of Egypt right here in Clare.

"UMAMR is hands down the best Egyptian restaurant in Clare. The food is always delicious and the service is excellent. I highly recommend the Koshari and the Mulukhiyah!"

- John Doe, Clare

Meet Our Chef

Our head chef, Amr Sayed, has over 40 years of experience in cooking Egyptian cuisine, and has brought his expertise to UMAMR Restaurant. He takes great pride in creating dishes that are both delicious and authentic, and he's always experimenting with new flavors and ingredients to keep our menu fresh and exciting.

Our Menu

At UMAMR Restaurant, we offer a wide range of dishes to suit all tastes, from traditional Egyptian favorites to fusion cuisine that blends flavors from around the world. Our menu features classics like Koshari, as well as unique dishes like our signature Kabab. There's something for everyone.

Visit Us

We're located in the heart of Ennis at 1 Z Street. We're open Monday-Saturday from 11am-8pm, and we offer both dine-in and takeout options. Come visit us and experience the best Egyptian food in Ireland!
